What is Manglic kundali ?

A Manglic kundali or Horoscope is a horoscope in which Mars (Mangal: Hindi name for mars) is placed in 1, 4, 7, 8 and 12 house, and this type of horoscope is called "Manglic Kundali". It is usually considered in the aspect of marraige life. It is checked while Match Making of horoscope before marraige. 7th house is the house spouce and marraige, and Mars has 3 aspects, 4th aspect, 7th aspect, 8th aspect.

So When Mars is present in 1st house it aspects 7th house of spouse (7th aspect from current position). When Mars is present in 4th house, it aspects 7th house (4th Aspect from current postion). When Mars is present in 7th House its actually present there. When Mars is presnt in 12th house it aspects 7th house (8th aspects from current position). Mars present in 8th house dsnt aspect 7th house, but 8th houes is house of death and mars can sudden death and decresae life span (provided not aspected by any good planet). Hence even mars in 8th house is avoided or called mangalic Horoscope.

If A horoscope is Manglic then there is tension between the Husband and wife's relation. Depending where mars is the effect may vary. but over all it causes Fights, Misunderstanding, Quarells, Police Cases, Court Cases, Voilance etc.
